Amaretto Pancakes

Description:

Sweet pancake that transforms an everyday pancake mix into something more exciting and flavorful without the need to add syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Member's Mark Buttermilk Pancake Mix
  • 3/4 Cups Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp Cinnammon
  • 1/2 Tsp Amaretto

Steps:

  1. Mix the wet ingredients: In a bowl, mix 3/4 cups almond milk, 1 tbsp oil, and 1/2 tsp amaretto.
  2. Combine dry ingredients: In a seperate bowl, stir together 1 cup of pancake mix, 1 1/2 tsp granulated sugar, 1 tbsp brown sugar, and 1/4 tsp cinnammon.
  3. Form the batter: Pour wet mix into dry mix and stir gently until moistened. Let rest for 5 min to hydrate the mix.
  4. Cook the pancake: Add 1/4 cup of batter to desired.
